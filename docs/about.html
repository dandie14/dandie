About Climbing

Mountain climbing is more than a sport—it's a journey. It demands strength, endurance, and spirit. Whether you’re scaling rocky cliffs or hiking peaceful trails, each climb brings a new story.

Types of Mountain Climbing

  1. Hiking/Trekking – Explore scenic trails at your own pace.
  2. Rock Climbing – Conquer vertical faces with gear and grit.
  3. Alpine Climbing – Brave icy heights and stunning views.
  4. Expedition Climbing – Reach peaks that few dare to dream of.

Essential Climbing Gear

  • Ropes, harnesses, helmets
  • Ice axes, crampons
  • Waterproof boots, thermal layers
  • Navigation tools: compass, GPS, maps
